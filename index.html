About Us

A Family Business Built on Trust

Service 4 Plumbing is a family-owned company that has been serving the San Francisco Bay Area for over 25 years. Formerly known as Service One Plumbing, Inc., we continue to be run and operated by the same dedicated owner and team.

Our goal has never changed: deliver outstanding plumbing service where the job gets done right the first time. We treat every home and business like it were our own.

Based in San Leandro, we serve clients across Oakland, Fremont, Hayward, Berkeley and the entire Bay Area for both residential and commercial needs.
